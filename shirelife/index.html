Activities and Events

In the town of hobbiton, you can enjoy an array of activities and events that will occur and be spread throughout all the Shire. Enjoy picnic parties, Gandalf's firework show, speak with traveling merchants, accept quests given by travelers. Enjoy the ordinary life of a hobbit or live adventurous. Enter into eating competitions, sword fighting tornaments, and engage in the search for 'The One Ring.'

Booking and Packages

Schedule and rent a hobbit hole of your liking. From Bagend to the home of Samwise. Buy package deals, VIP seats and menus, Event passes, and more. Give yourself the Shire Life experience you love and dream of. Be adventurous, take chances, live as ordinary or unexpected as you feel when the call for adventure comes your way.
