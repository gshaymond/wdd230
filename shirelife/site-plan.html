Overview

Purpose

Are you someone who loves adventure? Is there a fantasy world you long to experience in real life. For those that love the world of Middle Earth and its people, this can be your chance to live a life as a hobbit. In this fantasy world of magic and adventure, you can spend your nights in your hobbit hole, and spend the day living as a hobbit would. Have parties under the party tree of Hobbiton's fields, eat and dine six times a day at the Green Dragon Inn, enjoy Gandalf’s firework show, and more. You can live as simple as any ordinary hobbit, or do the unexpected and embark on adventures around Hobbiton. Become immersed in the world of Middle Earth, leave behind your world and answer this call to adventure.

Audience

Intended for those that love to be in a world of fantasy, that love Middle Earth. They can come to live a life like a hobbit would, and bask and enjoy the simple joys of a hobbit's life. For those that wish to eat seven meals a day; Breakfast, Second Breakfast, Elevenses, Luncheon, Afternoon Tea, Dinner, and Supper. Even join parties; under the Party Tree, or at the Green Dragon Inn. For those that would like to enjoy the life of ordinary hobbits, or not, and go on adventures around the shire, where there will be activity adventures.

Content

Home page

People will come to this website to have the experience of living as a hobbit in Hobbiton, sleeping inside a hobbit hole, and enjoying activities like living in the world of Middle Earth. Where there's rolling hills and beautiful views of the world around. There will be activities and events that will make their experience the most enjoyable they can have. They can be as ordinary or as unexpected as they see fit. They can make their own adventure with friends and family, allowing them to feel immersed in this world of magic and adventure.

Images for the Home page

Activities and Events

There will be many different activities and events that will happen throughout the Shire that attendees can participate or witness. There will be parties held in the fields under the Party Tree, and within the Green Dragon Inn, where there will be many actives and much to sing and dance to, and of course the famous speech of the birthdays that attend the event of the parties. As well as various activities that will be held in various locations throughout the Shire. There will be speed run activities, where people will compete to complete relay races, eating competitions, delivery runs etc. There will also be an activity of searching for the One Ring, where one person will be given the One Ring, everyone else will have to find who has it, the winner by the time the event ends, if they kept it from being found or found out who had it, will win a free event pass. Another activity will be foam weapon tournaments, each participant will be equipped with protecting armor and a foam weapon of their choice and will compete in one-on-one fights, winners win event passes and 'respect'. Among events, there will be plays performed of stories from the world of Middle Earth, from ancient lore to travelers' tales, to the stories of Bilbo and Frodo Baggins. And of course, there will also be the great and famous event where they can enjoy the magic of the wizard, Gandalf the Grey's firework show under the party tree and night sky with which you can enjoy an evening picnic. Other events include the appearance of certain races to show: from men of Gondor, Rohan; elves of Rivendell, Lothlorien, Mirkwood; dwarves of Moria, Erebor; and orcs of tower of Isengard. Each with an activity or special event that will take place. For example, the orcs may take someone captive or try to invade the Shire, and the people will defend themselves. The other races will have quests, which once completed will be rewarded with an unpurchaseable item. Some will be in caravans with purchasable items, and special goods.

Booking and Packages

The third and final page of this site will be the booking page, where the user can see what dates and times, they can pay to stay in Hobbiton. Including for how long they want to stay in their reservation, plus they can choose which hobbit hole they would like to stay in. Whether it be in the house of Samwise Gamgee or in under the hill in Bag End where they can explore and experience the life of a hobbit's most comfortable home. The users will be able to make reservations for VIP seats for special events such as the fireworks or the plays that will be held occasionally. There will also be events to which people can pay for to participate in, through package passes they may purchase, such as the foam weapon tournaments allowing them to put their metal to the test. Along with a private table in the Green Dragon Inn, with extraordinary a variety of options to choose from on the VIP menu. To participate in the quest events provided by the other races, they will have to buy the adventure package for them to be able to accept the quest and obtain a reward.
